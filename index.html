About Me

Hello! I am Aybike. A programmer based in London, UK. I am an enthusiastic software developer keen on learning, unlearning and relearning. A good team player who enjoys challenges and an analytical thinker with good problem-solving skills.

I studied computer engineering for my undergraduate and I recently completed a master's degree in games programming at City, University of London. I have experience with game engines and commonly used game development APIs.

I previously worked as a software engineer using C++ and Qt on development of a maritime simulator.

As a hobby, I enjoy participating in game jams. So far, I have completed and published games for 9 game jams, often collaborating with other developers. These projects allowed me to meet and work with people from different countries and disciplines, honing my soft skills as well as my technical skills.
